FLAVORS

ICE CREAM

Strawberry

STRAWBERRY

A classic. Experience a sweet but familiar taste with our strawberry ice cream. Made with the finest strawberries sourced, sweet and fruity to the core, we make sure our strawberry ice cream brings out the true original taste of strawberries with no artificial sweeteners to bring out that sweet and tarty flavor.

Chocolate

CHOCOLATE

You can’t go wrong with good old chocolate! Our Chocolate ice cream is made from the finest ingredients to ensure the consistency of its flavor. Just the right balance of chocolaty goodness and sweet and milky makes our chocolate ice cream one you won’t forget!

Cookies N' Cream

COOKIES N' CREAM

An explosion of chocolate and milky goodness, our Cookies and Cream ice cream brings you the familiar taste of sweet and sugary vanilla ice cream and a generous amount of chocolate biscuits that brings the crunch. A must have for those with an aching for a sugary cold snack.

Ube

UBE

A favorite among us Filipinos. Ube flavored ice cream is a natural delicacy for us in the Philippines and a must have for those visiting overseas. Our ice cream brings an earthy snack with that all familiar sweet and nutty goodness.

Mango

MANGO

Get a punch from our sweet and fruity Mango Ice Cream! Our mango flavored ice cream uses the finest and ripest mangoes to bring you this sweet and creamy tropical delight, a must have for those aching for some sweet and fruity delights.

Melon

MELON

Another tropical Delicacy. Have a unique experience with a rather unique flavor that you don’t find often. Our Melon ice cream gives you a new, refreshing, and sweet sensation that’s perfect for those hot summer days.

Buko

BUKO

A true tropical treat. A sweet and refreshing taste that mixes the tropical essence of buko and the creamy delight of ice cream. Our buko ice cream is another great companion for those hot sunny days that brings you the relaxing experience of a beach goer in the tropics.

Buko Pandan

BUKO PANDAN

Another Filipino Favorite. Our Buko Pandan ice cream mixes the sweet and fruity taste of young coconut with the aromatic and nutty taste of pandan, add some gelati, cream and sugar and you have a vibrant and explosive mix of sweet, nutty, and fruity delight.

Langka

LANGKA

A unique and distinct delicacy. Our Langa ice cream combines the fruity and unique flavor of ripe langka with the creamy goodness of ice cream. With the langka’s unique taste that tastes like a mix of pineapples, bananas and mangoes and smooth and creamy texture of ice cream comes a unique and refreshing flavor of ice cream to spice up your day and taste buds with this new and exotic delicacy.

Macapuno

MACAPUNO

Yet another Filipino favorite. Our Makapuno ice cream brings out the sweet and creamy taste of makapuno into frozen form. Mixed with the right amount of milk and bits of makapuno coconut meat brings a sweet, creamy, and exotic experience like no other.

Avocado

AVOCADO

A refreshing choice for those looking for a fruity snack. Our Avocado ice cream gives you a creamy, nutty, and fruity experience that brings out the natural flavors of Avocado mixed with the soothing and refreshing texture of ice cream to bring you this unique and fruity delight.

Cheese

CHEESE

A creamy and cheesy delight. Cheese brought to frozen form, our Cheese ice cream combines the creamy and savory taste of cheese with smooth and refreshing texture of ice cream into savory mix sure to impress cheese lovers looking for a cold and refreshing snack.
